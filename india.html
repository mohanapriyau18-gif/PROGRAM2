India - 10 Important Points

  1. India is one of the oldest civilizations in the world.
  2. The capital of India is New Delhi.
  3. India is the seventh largest country by land area.
  4. It is the most populous country in the world.
  5. India has 28 states and 8 union territories.
  6. The national language is Hindi, but many languages are spoken.
  7. India is famous for its culture, traditions, and festivals.
  8. The national animal of India is the Tiger.
  9. India is known for historical monuments like the Taj Mahal.
  10. The Indian national flag has three colors: saffron, white, and green.
