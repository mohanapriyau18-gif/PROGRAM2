United States of America - 10 Important Points

  1. The United States of America is commonly called the USA.
  2. The capital of the USA is Washington, D.C.
  3. The USA has 50 states.
  4. It is the third largest country in the world by land area.
  5. The USA has one of the largest economies in the world.
  6. English is the most widely spoken language in the USA.
  7. The national currency of the USA is the US Dollar.
  8. The USA is known for its technological and scientific advancements.
  9. Famous landmarks include the Statue of Liberty and the White House.
  10. The national animal of the USA is the Bald Eagle.
